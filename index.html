Resources and tools used for the Codemotion'13 panel "Undiscovered Country: How To Get Started Exploring Big Data".

About the Panel.

Big Data is a Big Topic, but if you don’t have a PhD or if you’ve never worked at a supercomputer, how do you get started? During this session four ladies will each present a rapid 10-minute intro with a practical approach for big data beginners. We’ll discuss data visualization, APIs, Hadoop, and open datasets. And we’ll use demos and live coding to dispell some of the mystery of data-crunching vodoo. Don’t think you’re ready? Moderators Johanna Brewer and Amélie Anglade will wrap-up by asking the speakers to share their first-time experiences in this undiscovered country.
